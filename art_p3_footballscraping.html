Football Games Results Web Scraping

Football, being one of if not the most popular sport in the world, has quite a few adepts in the Data Science sphere. When a colleague asked me to get him the information for several leagues in the last two seasons I knew I could count on the open source community to have something to guide me in the right direction.

Using Owen Seymour's ScraperFC we can easily get the results for whole regular seasons at a time! I used the library's FBRef module that lets us get results for the most popular leagues easily.
